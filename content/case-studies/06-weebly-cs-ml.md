Then redesigned the customer support internal tool around agent workflows with machine-learning-powered call prediction and upsell surfacing, lifting upsell conversion from 10% to 25%, reducing average handle time from 10 to 8 minutes, and generating ~$1.2M in incremental annualized revenue.
