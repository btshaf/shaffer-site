About 20% of users hitting the hotel detail page were terminating on a "no availability" screen with no real path forward. Most teams treat that state as an inventory problem. It isn't. It's a moment of unusually high purchase intent that the previous design was throwing away. Rebuilding that screen as a recovery surface, with confirmed availability and live pricing on nearby comparable hotels, converted 30% of previously lost users back into the booking funnel and recovered ~$14M in annualized bookings.
