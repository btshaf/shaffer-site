Diagnosed a "no availability" page where roughly 20% of users were leaving the booking funnel after their exact query returned no results. Redesigned it to surface nearby hotels with confirmed availability and pricing, treating the user's underlying intent (book a trip) as the real question. Recovered 30% of the previously lost users and turned the pattern into a repeatable funnel-recovery framework across other flows on a multibillion-dollar booking platform.
