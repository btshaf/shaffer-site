Joined Solo Global as Product Lead with no shipped product. Behavioral research surfaced that solopreneurs concentrate spending in two or three business categories per user, which became the basis for a category-personalized rewards model on a 1.5% interchange margin where the standard partner-subsidy playbook couldn't survive. Modeled break-even at 20% category concentration; production usage came in at 15% and the model held, with personalization driving a 10% lift in debit card creation.
