Recommended killing Square Pay's planned in-person expansion in early 2020 after COVID-driven tap-to-pay growth (~200%) broke its core QR-code UX assumption and Cash App Pay was building a stronger answer to the same problem. Made the case to Square's seller leadership against my own previously-championed roadmap, redirecting roughly 24 engineer-months across two teams to higher-leverage work and avoiding internal competition with a sibling product.
